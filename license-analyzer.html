Case Study: License Analyzer

The Problem

As Open Source is increasingly used to build modern software, license concerns can affect proprietary development. License Analyzer's goal is to answer the question, "What licenses are present in my product?" This allows product owners to mitigate an unfriendly license terms or considerations prior to launch.

Input: package.json

Output: Visualization of metadata, license(s), version(s), and links for further exploration as needed.
